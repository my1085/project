About Us

EcoSoles was founded in 2007 by Emily Carter, a marine biologist turned entrepreneur, and Alex Torres, a former industrial designer passionate about eco-friendly innovation. After witnessing firsthand the devastating effects of plastic pollution during a research trip to the Pacific Ocean, Emily became determined to create a product that would address environmental degradation. Partnering with Alex, they decided to start with something universal: shoes.

Starting in small eco-markets, EcoSoles now ships to over 50 countries and partners with major retailers like REI and Nordstrom. EcoSoles has also now removed over 5 million pounds of plastic from oceans and planted 1 million trees.
