Policies

Flexible Payment Plans:

We offer interest-free installment plans for purchases over $100. Customers can split payments into three to six monthly installments.



Buy-Back Program Incentives:

Customers can trade in their old EcoSoles products for store credit toward their next purchase. The returned shoes are then recycled or refurbished.



Carbon-Neutral Shipping Policy:

All orders are shipped using carbon-neutral methods. EcoSoles offsets the carbon emissions from shipping by investing in reforestation projects, renewable energy, or other verified offset programs.



Reusable Packaging Initiative:

Orders are delivered in reusable, returnable packaging. Customers can either keep the packaging for a small fee or return it for reuse at no additional cost.



Hassle-Free 30-Day Returns:

Customers can return or exchange products within 30 days of purchase if they are unworn, in their original condition, and accompanied by proof of purchase.
